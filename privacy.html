Privacy Policy

Grass Factory · Last updated: May 19, 2026

Overview

Grass Factory is an offline single-player game. This Privacy Policy explains how the game handles information when you install and play it.

Developer account and contact: hengbozail@163.com.

Information We Collect

Grass Factory does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or other personal profile information.

The game may store gameplay information locally on your device, such as unlocked levels, best star ratings, best completion times, music settings, and sound effects settings.

Local Storage

Gameplay progress and settings are saved on your device so the game can remember your progress after you close and reopen it. This local data is not sent to our own server.

You can remove locally stored game data by deleting the app from your device.

Advertising and Tracking

The app may display advertising if ad features are enabled in the distributed build. If advertising partners request access to the device advertising identifier, iOS will ask for your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt where required.

If you do not allow tracking, the game can still be played. Advertising partners may still show contextual or non-personalized ads depending on their own policies and Apple's rules.

Children's Privacy

Grass Factory is designed as a casual game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so we can review the request.

Third-Party Services

The game may include third-party SDKs for security, analytics, or advertising support. These services may process limited technical information according to their own privacy policies and applicable platform rules.

We do not operate a user account system or a game server for Grass Factory.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share gameplay progress from our own server because the game stores progress locally and does not require a server account.

Security

We use reasonable measures appropriate for an offline casual game to protect the app experience. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and you should keep your device and operating system up to date.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or Grass Factory, contact the developer account at hengbozail@163.com.
